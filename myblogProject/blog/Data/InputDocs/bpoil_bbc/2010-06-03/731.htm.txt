Is BP spill hurting UK reputation in the USA ? 
As the BP oil spill threatens to cause immense economic and environmental damage in the Gulf of Mexico , the reputation of BP in America at an all-time low . 
Giles Dilnot reports on whether the spill is affecting the image of other British businesses in the USA , and gets the thoughts of Carol Browner , presidential advisor on energy and climate , and Simon Middleton , branding expert and author .
