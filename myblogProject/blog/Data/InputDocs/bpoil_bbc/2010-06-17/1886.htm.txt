BP CEO Tony Hayward grilled by Congress over oil spill The pressure on BP in the US has continued as the oil company 's beleaguered chief executive , Tony Hayward was given a working over by a US Congressional Committee . 
He was accused of `` astonishing complacency '' and of cutting corners to save money , among a number of other major criticisms and complaints . 
During the course of the morning , Mr Hayward faced attacks from both politicians and the audience .
